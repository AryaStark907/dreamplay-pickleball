🏓 MATCH FORMAT

One Game to 15 — Win by 2

Teams play a single extended game. The first team to reach 15 points wins, but must lead by at least 2 points.

  • Typical game duration: 15–22 minutes
  • If tied at 16–16, first to 17 wins (even by 1 point) — hard cap
  • Scores above 15 are only valid if the winning team leads by 2, OR the score reaches 17
  • Example: 15–13 ✓ · 15–14 ✗ (not win by 2) · 17–16 ✓ (cap)
⚡ Score Entry Rule: If you enter 15 as a team's score, the opponent's score must be 13 or lower. If the score is 17, the opponent's score must be 16 or lower.
📅 SCHEDULE

Group Stage: Monday–Thursday · 12:30 PM – 8:30 PM

  • Each match slot: 30 minutes (match time)
  • Switch/buffer time: 10 minutes between matches
  • Total slot per match: 40 minutes

Finals Day (Friday): Quarter Finals → Semi Finals → Final starting 11:30 AM

🏆 ADVANCEMENT TO QUARTER FINALS

8 teams advance to QF from each category (Beginner & Inter+Adv):

  • Top 5 teams — The 1st place team from each of the 5 groups advances directly to the Quarter Finals
  • Next best 3 — From all remaining teams across the 5 groups, the top 3 by standings also advance

Top 3 selection from remaining teams is decided by standings (Points → Wins → Points Difference) or by the Dreamplay team.

📝 SCORE SUBMISSION
  • Either team can submit the score after a match
  • Enter your name when submitting — scores are tracked with submitter info
  • Scores can be corrected once (max 2 submissions per match)
  • After 2 submissions, scores are locked — contact Admin for any further changes
  • Submitting all zeros will reset a match score
